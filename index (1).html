About Finn Leary

From skinny kid to athlete, coach and fighter.

I grew up the skinny kid playing rugby league — always athletic, but always behind everyone else in size. Training started as a way to survive on the field and build some confidence. At 19 I started Muay Thai, and six years later it's completely changed how I see training: discipline, resilience, and moving with purpose, not just chasing size.

You don't need to be big.
You need to be powerful.

Beyond general strength coaching, I bring a sports science background and hands-on coaching experience across rugby league and Muay Thai — so if you're an athlete looking to hit harder, move faster, or get an edge in competition, that specialist work is built in too. Every program is tailored to the person, their goals, schedule, and the equipment they've got access to, whether that's a full gym or a home setup.

I coach both in person on the Sunshine Coast and online worldwide, so distance is never the barrier — only the work is.
